Elon Musk

Elon Musk was South African-born American entrepreneur, investor, and engineer.

He was born in Pretoria, South Africa on June 28, 1971.

Contributions & Dates:

  • Founded SpaceX (2002)
  • Founded Tesla, Inc. (2003)
  • Founded Neuralink (2016)

Developed reusable rockets and electric cars.
