Elon Musk

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He is an entrepreneur and is known for his space exploration, electric vehicles, and neurotechnology. He founded/co-founded SpaceX and Tesla. In 2002, he founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars. SpaceX's development of reusable rockets has made space travel more economically important. Tesla, founded in 2003, has been making electric vehicle movement. His other ventures include Neuralink, founded in 2016, devloped brain-machine interfaces. The Boring Company, also founded in 2016, focuses on tunnel construction and infrastructure. The Boring Company's goal was to reduce urban traffic. Overall, Elon Musk's has advanced technology and computer science.
