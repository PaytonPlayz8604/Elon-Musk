Elon Musk

He was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He is South African-born American entrepreneur, investor, and engineer. He is known for his space exploration, electric vehicles, and neurotechnology. In 2002, he founded SpaceX. During that time, he had a goal of reducing space transportation costs. Additionally, SpaceX's development of reusable rockets made space travel cheaper. Tesla, founded in 2003, has been making electric vehicles. Neuralink, founded in 2016, developed brain-machine interfaces. The Boring Company, also founded in 2016, focuses on tunnel construction and infrastructure. The Boring Company's goal was to reduce urban traffic.

Sources: Forbes and Tesla.
